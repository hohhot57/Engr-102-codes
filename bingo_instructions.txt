Howdy! Welcome to Bingo! The rules are simple, run the program, and follow the prompt on screen. To play/continue enter y, to stop enter n. The game will display your board after every turn as well as how many turns it took to get a BINGO. A BINGO is achieved by connecting 5 digits in a row based on the bingo number called. You can even compete with friends by comparing scores, a lower score is better! Have fun!
